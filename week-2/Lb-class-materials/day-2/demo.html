Introduction to ARIA and Web Accessibility

Web accessibility ensures that websites, tools, and technologies are designed and developed so that people with disabilities can use them. ARIA helps with this.

ARIA attributes provide essential information to assistive technologies, enhancing the web experience for users with disabilities.
